About Us

Egyptian producer and exporter of horticultural crops.

Who we are

We are an Egyptian company specialized in the growing and exporting of horticulture crops in accordance to international food safety and quality standards. Our farms are located in Wadi Alnatrun and managed with the most efficient elements through an integrated food safety and quality management system that complies with international standards of the food industry. We have acquired accreditation such as Global GAP Certification. We are serving the global market professionally and we have clients around the world.
